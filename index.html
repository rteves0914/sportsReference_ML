Who will be in the next group of inductees into the Hall of Fame?

The Hall of Fame is one of the greatest accomplishments an individual player can receive. There are currently 333 individuals in the Major League Baseball Hall of Fame, which is located in Cooperstown, NY. Of these 333, 235 of them are former players and 35 of them are former Negro League players. The remaining 63 people are umpires, executives, and coaches. The first Hall of Fame class was inducted in 1936 and included legends such as Babe Ruth, Ty Cobb, and Walter Johnson.


Using Machine Learning regression analysis, we can predict whether or not a player is on a hall of fame trajectory. Using these tools, I have created a model that trains on MLB players since 1876, and shows the career stats of any player from any decade.
